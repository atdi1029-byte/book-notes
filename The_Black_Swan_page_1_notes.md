# Title Page

**The Black Swan: The Impact of the Highly Improbable**

Author: **Nassim Nicholas Taleb**

- Core theme: exploration of **highly improbable events** and their outsized impact on history, finance, and human affairs
- The **black swan** metaphor: an event that appears impossible until it occurs, then seems inevitable in retrospect
- Foundational concept for understanding risk, uncertainty, and the limits of prediction
